ANSOFF Matrix

Plan Your Growth Strategy

What is the ANSOFF Matrix?

The ANSOFF Matrix is a strategic planning tool that helps you identify and evaluate growth opportunities for your content business. It maps out four key growth strategies based on whether you're working with existing or new products/markets.

For content creators, this means deciding whether to double down on your current audience (Market Penetration), reach new audiences with existing content (Market Development), create new content types for your audience (Product Development), or venture into entirely new areas (Diversification).

Market Penetration

Grow with existing audience + existing products

Market Development

Reach new audiences with existing products

Product Development

Create new products for existing audience

Diversification

New products for new audiences (higher risk)

Progress Step 1 of 6

Step 1: Your Current Situation

Let's start by understanding your current business position.

Example: Product: "Weekly newsletter about AI tools" | Audience: "SaaS founders and marketing professionals"

Step 2: Market Penetration

How can you get MORE from your EXISTING audience with your CURRENT products?

Example: "Increase upload frequency from weekly to 3x/week" | "Launch a Patreon for super fans" | "Optimize thumbnails for higher CTR"

Step 3: Market Development

How can you reach NEW audiences with your EXISTING products?

Example: "Cross-post to LinkedIn for B2B audience" | "Create short-form versions for TikTok/Reels" | "Partner with creators in adjacent niches"

Step 4: Product Development

What NEW products can you create for your EXISTING audience?

Example: "Turn best videos into an online course" | "Create a paid community/Slack group" | "Develop productivity templates"

Step 5: Diversification

What NEW products could you create for NEW audiences? (Higher risk, but potentially higher reward)

Example: "Launch a second channel about fitness (if main is business)" | "Build a software tool for a different market" | "Start consulting services"
