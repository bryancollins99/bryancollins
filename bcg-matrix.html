What is the BCG Matrix?

The BCG Growth-Share Matrix helps you categorize your products/content into 4 quadrants based on market growth (potential) and market share (current performance).

For content creators, this helps you decide which content/products to invest in, maintain, develop, or discontinue. It's about allocating your limited time and resources strategically.
