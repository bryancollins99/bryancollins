Why Most Freelancers Underprice

Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting about taxes (25-35%)
  • Not accounting for unbillable time
  • Ignoring business expenses
  • Comparing to employee salaries (apples to oranges)
  • Pricing based on fear, not value

Reality Check

  • 50% of time is typically non-billable
  • You need emergency savings buffer
  • No paid vacation = factor it in
  • Health insurance, retirement = your responsibility
  • Higher rates = better clients
