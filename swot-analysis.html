What is SWOT Analysis?

SWOT Analysis is a strategic planning framework that helps you identify internal and external factors affecting your business. It stands for Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats.

For content creators, this tool helps you understand what you're doing well (Strengths), where you need to improve (Weaknesses), what external trends you can leverage (Opportunities), and what challenges you need to prepare for (Threats).
