MY STORY

From Writer to Creator Strategist

I started my journey as a writer, fascinated by the creative process and how people bring ideas to life. Over the years, I wrote several books on creativity, productivity, and the power of doing meaningful work.

But I noticed a gap: while there were plenty of resources on the creative side, very few creators were thinking strategically about their businesses. Most were talented artists, writers, or makers who struggled with the business fundamentals.

That's when I started adapting MBA frameworks—tools like SWOT analysis, ANSOFF Matrix, and Porter's Five Forces—specifically for creators. I asked: "What would these strategic tools look like if they were designed for creators, not corporations?"

Today, I combine my love of writing with strategic thinking to help creators build sustainable businesses. I create free tools, write practical guides, and share insights through my newsletter and YouTube channel.

My mission is simple: help creators think strategically so they can build businesses that last.
